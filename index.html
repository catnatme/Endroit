Our Passion for French Cuisine

Welcome to L'Endroit, a culinary haven located in the picturesque town of 24200 Sarlat-la-Canéda. Our restaurant is born from a deep love for the rich gastronomic heritage of the Périgord region.

We are committed to sourcing the freshest, highest-quality local ingredients to craft authentic and innovative French dishes. Our menu is a celebration of seasonal produce and regional specialties, prepared with skill and passion.

At L'Endroit, we believe that dining is more than just a meal; it's an experience. Our warm and inviting atmosphere is designed to make you feel at home, whether you're joining us for a relaxed lunch or an elegant dinner. We strive to create memorable moments for every guest, combining exceptional food with attentive service.

Join us and embark on a culinary journey that captures the essence of French dining in Sarlat-la-Canéda. We look forward to welcoming you.
